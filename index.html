Introduction.

SML(Society For Managment Learning)

  • Register Under the aegis of Division of Student Welfare,Lovely Professional University
  • Organization was startes in the year 2012 and working effectively till now. Started by students at Mittal School of Business.
  • Organization's motive is to make students aware of social responsiblilties, works to develop the quality of managment through various events and workshops.

Mission.

Shape the future by creating an effectively responsible society for learning managment qualities throughout the world and making human beings socially responsible.

Vision.

To become the most respected and successful student organization diversifying in universities and schools, delighting students by orgaization events that inspire mind and chnage life with the best managment knowledge and the best people.
